# Running Plan — Week (Earliest Day First)

**Aligned with:** WEEKLY_TRACKER_PRINT.md, DAILY_ACTIVITY_GUIDE.md.  
**Pace/HR:** Easy = conversational, 65–75% max HR; Tempo = comfortably hard, 80–88%; Hard = 88–95%. Use Whoop zones if available.

---

## Why this order (short analysis)

| Day | Run type | Role in the week |
|-----|----------|-------------------|
| **Mon** | Easy | First day of the plan; gentle start, recovery from weekend long run or rest. No heavy load so you can do optional Gym A or yoga. |
| **Tue** | Tempo | First quality day; 24 h after easy run, legs are fresh enough for “comfortably hard” work. |
| **Wed** | — | Gym (upper). No run so legs recover before Thursday intervals. |
| **Thu** | Intervals | Second quality day; 48 h after tempo, upper body taxed yesterday so run focus is legs. |
| **Fri** | — | Gym (full body). No run; recovery before Saturday long run. |
| **Sat** | Long easy | Long run when you have time and one full rest day (Fri gym, not running) behind you. |
| **Sun** | Hills or yoga | Either power/leg stimulus (hills) or active recovery (yoga) to close the week without stacking two long runs back-to-back. |

**Principle:** Easy → quality (tempo) → rest (gym) → quality (intervals) → rest (gym) → long → hills/recovery. Hard run days are separated by at least one easy or non-running day.

---

## Monday — Easy run (Day 1)

**Purpose:** Start the week easy; recovery; habit and steps.

| Phase | What to do | Duration | Pace / HR |
|-------|------------|----------|-----------|
| **Warm-up** | Walk 2–3 min, then easy jog | 3–5 min | Very easy; HR building to 65–72% |
| **Main** | Easy run outside | 25–35 min | Conversational; nose-breathing possible. HR 65–75% max. No surges. |
| **Cool-down** | Easy jog → walk | 3–5 min | Let HR come down |

**Total:** ~30–45 min.  
**Optional after:** Gym A (lower + core) or yoga (video).

---

## Tuesday — Tempo run (Day 2)

**Purpose:** Lactate threshold; “comfortably hard”; first quality session of the week.

| Phase | What to do | Duration | Pace / HR |
|-------|------------|----------|-----------|
| **Warm-up** | Easy jog outside | 10 min | Easy; HR 65–72% |
| **Main** | Tempo block | 20–25 min | Comfortably hard — short sentences only. HR 80–88% max. Even effort. |
| **Cool-down** | Easy jog | 8–10 min | Easy; HR back to 65–75% |

**Total:** ~40 min.  
**No run tomorrow:** Wednesday is gym (upper).

---

## Wednesday — No run (Gym day)

**Running:** Rest from running.  
**Activity:** Gym B — Upper + core (~45–55 min).  
**Why:** Lets legs recover before Thursday intervals; upper body gets full focus.

---

## Thursday — Intervals (Day 3)

**Purpose:** High intensity; power, leg tone, metabolism; second quality session.

| Phase | What to do | Duration | Pace / HR |
|-------|------------|----------|-----------|
| **Warm-up** | Easy jog | 10 min | Easy; HR 65–72% |
| **Strides** | 4 × 20–30 s relaxed fast, full recovery | ~3 min | Smooth, not max |
| **Main** | 8 × 1 min hard / 1 min easy | 16 min | **Hard:** can’t chat; HR 88–95%. **Easy:** jog or walk; full recovery. |
| **Cool-down** | Easy jog | 8–10 min | Easy; HR down |

**Total:** ~35–40 min.  
**Alternative:** 6–8 × 400 m hard with 90 s walk/jog instead of 1 min/1 min.  
**No run tomorrow:** Friday is gym (full body).

---

## Friday — No run (Gym day)

**Running:** Rest from running.  
**Activity:** Gym C — Full body (~40–50 min).  
**Why:** Recovery before Saturday long run; full body strength without adding run volume.

---

## Saturday — Long easy run (Day 4)

**Purpose:** Base; fat oxidation; time on feet; mental.

| Phase | What to do | Duration | Pace / HR |
|-------|------------|----------|-----------|
| **Warm-up** | Walk 2 min, easy jog | 5–8 min | Very easy |
| **Main** | Long easy run outside | 60–75 min | Conversational; even pace. HR 65–75% max. Flat to rolling. No surges. |
| **Cool-down** | Easy jog → walk | 5–8 min | Let HR come down |

**Total:** ~70–90 min.  
**Route:** Prefer flat to rolling; outside.

---

## Sunday — Hills or yoga (Day 5 or recovery)

**Option A — Hills**

**Purpose:** Glutes, quads, power; high intensity without long duration.

| Phase | What to do | Duration | Pace / HR |
|-------|------------|----------|-----------|
| **Warm-up** | Easy jog | 10 min | Easy; HR 65–72% |
| **Main** | 8–10 × 60–90 s uphill | 16–25 min | **Uphill:** hard effort; HR high (88–95%). **Down:** jog or walk only — full recovery. |
| **Cool-down** | Easy jog | 8–10 min | Easy |

**Total:** ~40–50 min.

**Option B — Yoga**  
Follow your video (30–45 min). Counts as recovery; no run. Use if Whoop recovery is low or you prefer to close the week with mobility only.

## One-page running week (print-friendly)

| Day | Session | Total time | Key focus |
|-----|---------|------------|-----------|
| **Mon** | Easy run 25–35 min | ~30–45 min | Easy start; HR 65–75% |
| **Tue** | Tempo: 10 easy + 20–25 tempo + 8–10 easy | ~40 min | Comfortably hard; HR 80–88% |
| **Wed** | No run — Gym B (upper) | — | Legs rest |
| **Thu** | Intervals: 10 easy + 8×(1 hard / 1 easy) + 8–10 easy | ~35–40 min | Hard reps; HR 88–95% |
| **Fri** | No run — Gym C (full body) | — | Legs rest before long run |
| **Sat** | Long easy 60–75 min | ~70–90 min | Easy; HR 65–75% |
| **Sun** | Hills 8–10×60–90 s or yoga | ~40–50 min or 30–45 min | Power or recovery |

**Running volume (example):** ~4 runs, ~3–4 h total. Adjust long run or add/remove one easy run to match your goals and recovery (Whoop).
