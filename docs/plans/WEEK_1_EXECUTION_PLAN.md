## If-then rules (important)

- **If Whoop recovery is red:** remove hard session; do easy walk/run + yoga.
- **If strong hunger 2+ days in a row:** add 100-150 kcal (carbs around training).
- **If weight drops >0.5 kg/week + fatigue:** increase intake by 100-150 kcal/day.
- **If period symptoms worsen:** reduce intensity week and prioritize sleep/fuel.
