# Nutrition Plan 7 Days (Flexible, office + run compatible)

**Goal:** fat loss to ~49 kg with muscle retention, stable energy, and better recovery.  
**Calories:** average ~1500-1650/day (hard days up to ~1750).  
**Protein:** 100-120 g/day.

---

## Daily macro targets by day type

| Day type | Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| Easy/recovery day | 1400-1500 | 105-120 g | 120-150 g | 45-55 g |
| Strength day | 1500-1650 | 105-120 g | 140-180 g | 45-55 g |
| Hard run / long run day | 1600-1750 | 100-115 g | 180-230 g | 40-50 g |

---

## Meal building template (simple)

- **Breakfast:** protein + smart carbs + fruit
- **Lunch:** protein + carbs + vegetables + healthy fat
- **Snack:** protein-centered
- **Dinner:** protein + vegetables + carbs (less on easy day, more on hard day)

## Office food prep tips

- Cook protein in bulk 2x/week (chicken/turkey/fish portions by 120-150 g).
- Keep office staples: tuna packs, rice cups, nuts (small packs), fruit, yogurt.
- Put lunch container labels: `P` (protein), `C` (carb), `V` (veg), `F` (fat).

---

## Health-specific notes (important)

- With PCOS + unstable cycle and your training volume, avoid steep deficits.
- If cycle worsens, energy collapses, or recovery goes red several days: add 100-200 kcal (mostly carbs around training).
- Lipids focus: fibre daily (25-35 g), fatty fish 1-2x/week, less saturated fat from cheese/butter-heavy days.
