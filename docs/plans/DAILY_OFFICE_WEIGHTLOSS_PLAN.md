# Daily Office Weight-Loss Plan (8:30-17:00 workday)

**Goal:** become leaner/drier while staying strong, athletic, and healthy (PCOS + lipid history considered).  
**Style:** flexible templates, not rigid rules.

---

## Honest baseline

- You are not "broken" after 31. Most often this feeling is a mix of stress, sleep inconsistency, water retention, and body-composition drift.
- `Mesomorph` is not a useful planning variable. The useful variables are: calories, protein, training quality, sleep consistency, and weekly trend.
- Fast, aggressive cutting + hard running + frequent HIIT is the fastest way to lose muscle and cycle stability. We will not do that.

---

## Weekly structure (office-compatible)

| Day | Main training | Notes |
|-----|---------------|------|
| Mon | Easy run + mobility | Low stress start |
| Tue | Strength (upper + core) | Progressive overload |
| Wed | Office commute run (8 km one-way) + yoga | Easy/steady pace |
| Thu | Quality run (tempo or intervals) | Only 1 hard run |
| Fri | Strength (lower + core) | Moderate, no maxing |
| Sat | Long easy run 60-80 min | Aerobic base + fat oxidation |
| Sun | Yoga + 8-12k steps | Recovery day |

**If recovery is low (Whoop red/yellow + fatigue):** replace hard session with easy run/walk + yoga.

## Training details (simple and effective)

## Running intensity rules

- **80-90%** of total run time = easy conversational pace.
- **Hard sessions:** max **1-2 per week** (tempo/intervals/hills total).
- Office 8 km commute runs should usually be **easy**, not race efforts.

## Strength sessions (2x/week, optional 3rd light)

## Upper + core (Tue)
- Row 3x10-12  
- Push-ups or bench 3x8-12  
- Overhead press 3x8-10  
- Lat pull-down 2x10-12  
- Biceps + triceps superset 2x12  
- Plank + dead bug 2 rounds

## Lower + core (Fri)
- Squat (or goblet squat) 3x8-10  
- RDL 3x8-10  
- Step-up or split squat 2x10/leg  
- Hip thrust 2x10-12  
- Calf raises 2x15-20  
- Side plank + pallof press 2 rounds

**Rule:** keep 1-2 reps in reserve; clean form > ego weight (lumbar history).

---

## Daily non-negotiables

- **Protein:** 100-120 g/day.
- **Water:** 2.2-2.7 L/day.
- **Steps:** 8k minimum, 10k target.
- **Sleep:** fixed wake time, 7.5-8.5 h target.
- **5-10 min mobility/activation** daily (hips, ankles, thoracic, calves).

---

## 4-week expected outcome (realistic)

- **Weight trend:** about 1.0-2.0 kg down in 4 weeks (not linear).
- **Look:** less puffiness, tighter waist, better muscle definition.
- **Performance:** easier zone-2 running, better gym stability.

If no change after 14 days:
- reduce average intake by ~100 kcal/day **or**
- add 1 extra 20-30 min easy walk on 4 days/week.

Do not cut below recovery capacity (cycle and recovery come first).
